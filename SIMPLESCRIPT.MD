## What to do After an Volcanic Eruption Shorter

After a volcanic eruption, follow these steps to stay safe:

1.)Listen to authorities and evacuate if necessary.

2.)Protect your health with protective gear.

3.)Check for damage and report hazards.

4.)Be prepared for aftershocks.

5.)Stay informed about the latest news and information.

By taking these steps, you can help protect yourself and your community in the aftermath of a volcanic eruption.
